Privacy Policy

This site exists to help you protect your privacy. It would be hypocritical if the site itself didn't respect it.

1. No Tracking or Analytics

This website does not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixels, or any other third-party tracking scripts. I do not track your behavior across the site, I do not build a profile of your visits, and I do not share any data with advertisers.

2. No Cookies

This website does not set any cookies on your device. You will not see a cookie consent banner here because there is nothing to consent to.

3. Hosting and Server Logs

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like all web servers, GitHub automatically collects basic connection information (such as IP addresses and user agents) to serve the website files to your browser and prevent abuse. I do not have access to these raw logs, and GitHub's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy.

4. Contact Form

If you choose to use the contact form, your message is transmitted to and processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. Submitting the form sends your email address, selected reason, and message text to Formspree's servers before it is forwarded to me. Formspree's handling of that data is governed by their Privacy Policy. I only receive the information you explicitly provide in the form fields. I will never share your message or contact details with anyone else, and I will delete them after reviewing.

5. External Links

This site contains links to external products and services. While I carefully vet every recommendation, I do not control those external websites. Once you leave PrivacyChick.io, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective sites.
