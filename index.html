My Story

Real person.
Real recommendations.

I’m a gamer who started digging into data collection years ago and came to a simple conclusion: if you’re not paying for a product, you are the product. That made me wildly uncomfortable, so I started locking down my digital life.

I didn’t do it alone. My son adopted these tools easily, proving that when you introduce privacy early, the next generation just gets it. And my wife, who cares more about yoga than tech, uses them because she understands that her personal information is her most valuable commodity and she wants to control who has access to it.

If we can do it, you can too.
