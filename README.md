# Privacy Chick

**Your digital privacy, simplified.**

Privacy Chick is a personal, opinionated guide to protecting your digital life — written by a real person who got fed up feeling like his data was out of his control.

This site is not affiliated with any company. There are no ads, no affiliate links, and no financial relationships with any product recommended here. Everything on this site is something the author personally uses.

## Philosophy

Most privacy advice falls into one of two traps: it's either so technical it assumes a computer science degree, or so vague it never tells you what to actually do. Privacy Chick is an attempt to fill that gap — clear, specific, honest guidance that anyone can follow.

You don't need a technical background. You don't need to understand how encryption works. You just need clear instructions and a willingness to spend a few minutes making changes that will protect you for years.

## Technical notes

This is a fully static site — plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step required to view it, no server-side code, no database.

Topic pages are generated by `build_pages.py`. To regenerate all topic pages after editing that file:

```bash
python3 build_pages.py
```
